Popular Destinations / Kannur /Arakkal-Museum

Arakkal Museum is dedicated to the only Muslim royal family of Kerala, Arakkal Ali Rajas. The museum is located inside the Durbar Hall of the palace where the royal family lived and conducted gatherings with kings and nobles. Get an insight into their culture and lives through furniture, art, weapons and more displayed in the museum. Even if you are not a history buff, this place is among the best things to see in Kannur because of the palace. It has a majestic grandeur and elegance that immediately takes you in the past. The palace never fails to cause wide-mouthed wonder to its visitors.
