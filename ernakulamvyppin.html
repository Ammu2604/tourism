Popular Destinations / Ernakulam / Vyppin Island

Vypeen is one of the popular places to visit in Ernakulam in one day
and certainly one of the best islands in Kerala.
A short and beautiful ferry ride from the city will take to this serene island which is dotted with beaches,
backwaters and an array of restaurants and hotels.
A desktop worthy scenery away from the bustling environment is what makes
Vypeen island one of the best places to visit in Ernakulam for lovers.
